ABOUT

Medservice, Inc. is a premier provider of independent review services.

WHO WE ARE

  • Medservice was established in Las Vagas, CA and has developed a sophisticated platform, Peer-X-Change, to connect independent physician advisors nationwide with healthcare payers.
  • We contract with a nationwide panel of independent physician advisors to cover most specialties in most jurisdictions.
  • We focuses on Worker’s Comp, but our physician advisors are available to conduct all other types of reviews such as IMR's or closure reviews.
  • Medservice understands the time sensitive nature of medical peer review. Based on that foundation, we have developed a rush-system that consists of technological components and a dedicated team of Utilization Review Physicians who have volunteered to make themselves available on a rush basis.
